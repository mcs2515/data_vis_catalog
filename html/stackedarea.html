Stacked Area are useful for viewers to compare multiple variables that are changing over time. Similar to Area graphs except that Stacked area graphs show multiple data series starting from each point of the previous series. This creates multiple layers or stacks of data. The entire graph represents the total data plotted. Stacked Area graphs do not work for negative values as they convey whole numbers.
