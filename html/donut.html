Donut Chart

Mark:

Lines

Channel:

Angle

Donuts are essentially Pie charts with its center missing. What is left is the edge which in turn helps the viewer see the length of each slice. The length of each slice, also, makes it easier for the viewer to compare the slices from one to another. Donuts charts are space efficient and allows the creator to use the center as additional space for information to display.
