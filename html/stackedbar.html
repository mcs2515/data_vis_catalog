Stacked Bar charts show multiple datasets on top of each other. In this way, the graph shows how the larger category gets segmented into smaller categorical pieces. The graph, also, shows the relationship of each segment on the total amount. Too many segments may make it harder for the viewer to compare each segment and the graph becomes harder to read.


There are two types Stacked Bars:

Simple Stacked Bar: the total value is the sum of its smaller segments.

100% Stacked Bar: the total value is the sum of its smaller segments.
