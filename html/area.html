Area Graph

Channel:

Area

Area Graph is essentially a Line graph with the area below the line filled in with a color or texture. Similar to the Line graph, the Area graph displays how quantitative values change over time. Area graphs are used to show trends rather than showing specific values.


P.S. This graph is interactable.
